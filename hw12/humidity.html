Latitude vs. Humidity

This plot comes closer to showing some sort of correlation than the cloudiness and wind speed plots, but again, there's no strong correlation between distance from the equator and humidity. Interestingly, it does appear that humidity is higher on average near and below the equator, but this isn't enough to suggest a strong relationship between proximity to the equator and humidity.
