Does Latitude Matter?

Most of us take it for granted: the equator is the warmest part of the planet. But does it affect other aspects of the weather? In this project, we set out to find out, using a weather API, Python (including matplotlib and Seaborn) and any scientist's greatest asset, our own curiosity. The results largely confirmed what we already knew, but the charts sure do look pretty!
